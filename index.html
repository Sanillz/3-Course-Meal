Menu

Starters

Bruschetta

Ingredients: Tomatoes, Basil, Olive Oil, Garlic, Baguette.

Instructions: Toast the baguette slices, rub with garlic, top with tomatoes and basil, drizzle with olive oil.

Caesar Salad

Ingredients: Romaine Lettuce, Caesar Dressing, Croutons, Parmesan Cheese.

Instructions: Toss lettuce with dressing, top with croutons and cheese.

Main Course

Grilled Chicken with Veggies

Ingredients: Chicken Breast, Bell Peppers, Zucchini, Olive Oil, Spices.

Instructions: Season the chicken, grill with veggies until cooked through.

Pasta Primavera

Ingredients: Pasta, Bell Peppers, Zucchini, Olive Oil, Garlic, Parmesan Cheese.

Desserts

Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients: Dark Chocolate, Eggs, Cream, Sugar.

Instructions: Melt chocolate, fold in whipped cream and beaten eggs, chill before serving.

Cheesecake

Ingredients: Cream Cheese, Sugar, Eggs, Graham Cracker Crust.

Instructions: Blend cream cheese with sugar and eggs, pour into crust, bake and chill.
